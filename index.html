Biography

Damilola Alimi is an Engineer and tech community leader passionate about building and creating innovative solutions that drive positive change. Throughout her career, Damilola has demonstrated a strong commitment to excellence and a willingness to embrace new challenges. She has worked with several startups and currently works with Codemagic, a leading CI/CD platform for mobile applications.

Beyond her work in tech, Damilola is deeply committed to community building and mentoring aspiring tech professionals, particularly women. She has been a pivotal figure in several initiatives and currently serves as the Programs Manager for the Google Developer Group (GDG) Ibadan and an organizer for the largest Flutter conference in Africa, the FlutterBytes Conference.

Damilola graduated with a First Class degree in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Ibadan and her academic excellence and leadership prowess have earned her numerous accolades, including being selected as a Scholar of the Nigerian Higher Education Foundation (NHEF), emerging the National Winner of the CODET Engineering Project Competition, amongst many other awards.

Driven by her passion for advancing technology in Africa, Damilola aspires to become a leading Engineer. Her ultimate goal is to contribute to the growth and development of the tech ecosystem while continuing to inspire and empower others, particularly women, to pursue their dreams.
